Dumpling recipe


Description:

This dish, if cooked correctly, can be an explosion of flavor and juice that will make your mouth water from just the smell of it.

Ingredients:

  • dumpling wrap
  • dumpling filling (sold seperately)
  • a steamer (can buy from any good asian store)
  • a small bowl of water, about 1/3 cup

Steps:

  1. Take about 1/5 cup of filling (adjust as needed depending on wrapper size) and put it in the center of the dumpling wrapper
  2. fold the wrapper in half to cover the filling and wet your fingers to seal it
  3. put in steamer for however long you want
