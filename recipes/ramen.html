Instant ramen recipe



Description:

A noodle soup with varying mouth watering broth and noodles that easily go down.

DISCLAIMER! It will not look this good.


Ingredients:

  • Water
  • instant noodles packet from somewhere like shin ramen
  • pot and stove

Steps:

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Keep water at simmmer and add in the noodles and sauce from the packet
  3. Keep at boil for 2-3 minutes or until the noodles get soft
